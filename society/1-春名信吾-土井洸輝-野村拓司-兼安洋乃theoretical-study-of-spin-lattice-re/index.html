RESEARCH SUBJECT

The behavior of electrons governs many properties of materials. Therefore, understanding the behavior and properties of electrons in materials is important for uncovering the macroscopic nature of materials. In strongly correlated electron systems, in particular, interactions between electrons give rise to many fascinating phenomena, such as superconductivity, magnetic order, and charge order. In our study, we theoretically investigate the properties and ordered states of solids, as well as their underlying mechanisms, using quantum field theory and numericul simulations.
